About BaySpray

BaySpray is a San-Francisco-born spray paint label designed for writers, muralists and street artists who need color that survives fog, sun and salt air.

Our cans are built with low-drip valves, high-pigment formulas and a range of caps so you can switch from razor-sharp outlines to fat fills without changing brands.

BaySpray was created by a 19-year-old girl currently enrolled at San Francisco State University. She always felt that San Francisco's tough weather was never kind to any paint, so she decided to create her own brand specializing in difficult conditions.
